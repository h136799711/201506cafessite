Distribution and delegations

Global presence

Cafes Oquendo is based in the north of Spain in the middle of the Cantabrian coast, in Asturias, a province with a long tradition of fine coffee making.
We are a well established company with over twenty years of experience. On a national scale we are one of the leading companies in the hotel, food and catering and industry and we are now increasing and consolidating our presence in international markets.
